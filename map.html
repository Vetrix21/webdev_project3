Locations

Three locations important to me have been added into this map.

  • Armour Tringle — I am a member,, and on the executive board of, Armour Triangle. The house is where I live during the semester, and many memories and bonds have been made here.
  • Smith Park — In middle school, my friends and I went to a summer camp at this park. I remember biking past this tank every weekday, and after we outgrew the camp we used the park as a base whenever we hung out, meeting up here before walking around the neighborhood looking for somewhere to sit and eat.
  • Bottom Lounge — My favorite music venue in Chicago. I enjoy both attending concerts and playing them at this venue; with my band I have played here twice, and this is my favorite stage that I have played on. The venue itself is a good vibe for metal, and the food is also good here.
